## Words

### Preserve

- 为防止事物被破坏、损害或变质而保护、保留它原本的状态

A new art museum is being built to preserve the cultural heritage of the local community.

Making sauerkraut is a good way to preserve cabbage.

### Conserve

- 通过管理、利用、节约，从而达到保护的目的
- 多用于谈论保护自然和生态环境、减少浪费、节约能源的语境中

This area is severely affected by drought. They’re doing their best to conserve water.

### Reserve

- 专门保留、预留
- 预定
- n. 动植物、生态环境的保护区，如，wildlife reserves

I’m afraid the front seats are reserved for our staff. Would you mind sitting somewhere else?

I’d like to reserve a table at 7pm, please.

### [Remain](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english-chinese-simplified/remain)

- v. to stay in the same place or in the same condition
- to continue to exist when other parts or other things no longer exist

It remains a secret.

The doctor ordered him to remain in bed for a few days.

Only a few hundred of these animals remain today.
